# Vannevar Bush

## Summary

American engineer who proposed the **Memex** in 1945 — a personal knowledge store with associative trails between documents. A conceptual ancestor of the LLM Wiki pattern.

## Key facts

- Described Memex as private and actively curated
- Emphasized connections between documents as valuable as the documents themselves
- Could not solve the maintenance problem — now addressed by LLM agents
