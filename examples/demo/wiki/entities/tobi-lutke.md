# Tobi Lutke

## Who

Creator of [qmd](https://github.com/tobi/qmd), a local hybrid search engine for markdown knowledge bases.

## Relevance

qmd addresses the scale limit of pure lexical search in compounding wikis — the retrieval layer Karpathy's pattern defers until ~100 sources.
