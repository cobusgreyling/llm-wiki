# Andrej Karpathy

## Summary

AI researcher who articulated the **LLM Wiki** pattern — using LLM agents to maintain a compounding personal knowledge base instead of relying on per-query RAG retrieval.

## Key facts

- Published the LLM Wiki idea as a gist (April 2026)
- Coined the analogy: *"Obsidian is the IDE; the LLM is the programmer; the wiki is the codebase."*
