# Wiki Lint

## Definition

Periodic health-check of the wiki: contradictions between pages, stale claims superseded by newer sources, orphan pages, missing entity pages, broken cross-references, and data gaps.

## Why it matters

As the wiki grows, drift accumulates. Lint keeps the compounding artifact trustworthy and surfaces new questions to investigate.
