# Memex

## Definition

Vannevar Bush's 1945 vision of a personal, curated knowledge store with **associative trails** linking documents. Private and actively maintained — closer to LLM Wiki than the web became.

## Why it matters

The Memex anticipated compounding personal knowledge bases. Bush's unsolved problem — who does the maintenance — is what LLM agents now handle.
