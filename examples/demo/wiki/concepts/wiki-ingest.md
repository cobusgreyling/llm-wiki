# Wiki Ingest

## Definition

The operation of processing a new raw source: read it, discuss takeaways, write a source summary, update entity/concept pages, revise synthesis, flag contradictions, update index, append to log.

## Why it matters

A single ingest typically touches **10–15 wiki pages**. This is where compounding happens — knowledge is compiled into the graph, not left in raw files.
